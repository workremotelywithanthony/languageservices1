Our Specialization

At Sacasta Tech, we specialize in delivering seamless remote language solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of businesses, institutions, and individuals.

  • Medical Interpretation

    • Supporting doctors, nurses, and patients to communicate accurately and compassionately in healthcare settings.

  • Legal Interpretation

    • Ensuring that everyone fully understands proceedings, documents, and conversations in legal and judicial environments.

  • Business & Corporate Interpreting

    • Helping companies bridge language gaps during meetings, negotiations, training sessions, and global collaborations.

  • Community Interpreting

    • Assisting in schools, social services, and community programs to ensure equal access and participation for all.

  • Conference Interpreting

    • Providing real-time interpreting at conferences, seminars, and workshops to connect international participants smoothly.

  • Emergency & Crisis Interpreting

    • Offering fast, accurate support in urgent situations such as emergency calls, disaster response, or critical services.

  • Technical & Industry-Specific Interpreting

    • Handling specialized terminology and concepts in fields like IT, engineering, finance, and manufacturing.

  • Video Remote Interpreting (VRI) / Over-the-Phone Interpreting (OPI)

    • Quick and reliable interpreting done entirely over the phone or through secure video, giving clients the benefit of both voice and visual cues.
